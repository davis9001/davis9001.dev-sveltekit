A multiplayer browser game built for the *Space community. Players navigate a
sphere world, convert hostile ships into allied satellites, and collaborate to
decode hidden patterns. Features include real-time multiplayer, power-ups
(shields, speed boost, multi-shot), and Discord login integration.
