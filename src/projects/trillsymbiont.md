Trill Symbiont is an interactive generative ambient music tool featuring a
Circle of Fifths-based music grid, drum sequencer, and a physics scene that
correlates with tempo. Users can activate squares on the music grid organized
by musical intervals to create evolving ambient soundscapes, with full control
over oscillator waveforms, LFO modulation, and decay settings.
